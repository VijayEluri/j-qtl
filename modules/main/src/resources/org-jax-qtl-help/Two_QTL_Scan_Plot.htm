Two QTL Scan Plot

J/qtl's Two QTL Scan Plot is analogous to R/qtl's plot.scantwo function. Right-clicking on the graph will bring up a context menu that allows you to display an Effect Plot for the marker positions that were clicked.

 

Two QTL Scan Plot Settings:

The Two QTL Scan Plot allows you to choose the scan results to plot as well as what type of LOD scores should be plotted in the upper and lower triangles of the graph.
